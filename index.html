My Treking Journey

Nainital Tiffin Top Trek

When I visited India last year, I went on this trek with some of my friends. It was my first time doing trekking, and I never imagined that a 2 hour long trek would be this fun. after such a long journey we were able to watch such a beautiful scenery. Honestly one of my unforgettable travel experience. One thing I would like to suggest to people who have never been on this trek before, is you can schedule a horse riding to enjoy this trek.

Sem Mukhem Temple Trek

This was my second ever trek, I went on this trek with my family and my cousins. This place is located in my parents hometown. This place has such a beautiful view, since this place is surrounded by other mountains. It is such a calm and peaceful place. One advice I would give to people who have never been to this place is that, even though the security is pretty good you might get to see wild animals if lucky enough.
